About Clout Chaserz

Clout Chaserz is a lifestyle brand built around modern ambition. It curates products, aesthetics, and ideas for people who move with intent — creators, thinkers, and quiet grinders chasing more than just attention.

Founded by John Jeiven Tejuco, Clout Chaserz was created from a desire to merge culture, discipline, and design into everyday essentials. The brand represents focus over noise, taste over trends, and consistency over shortcuts.

From minimalist tools to intentional lifestyle pieces, Clout Chaserz exists for those who understand that real clout is built — not chased.
